 The series of “Computing π-Conjugated Compounds" (CπC) symposia offers an informal forum for the discussion of the latest research results in the domain of π-conjugated systems, covering the fields of functional materials, photochemistry, biochemistry, theory and computation. The CπC symposium series started in 2010 in Valencia and have been taking place since then on a yearly basis. In 2017, we have created a society to support the activity of the network and help in the organization of the symposium. Everyone is welcome to join and become a member!
